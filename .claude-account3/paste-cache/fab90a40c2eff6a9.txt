Accent colors
The source also exposes brighter accent values like #ff8000, #d500ff, and #ff8001. Those do appear in the CSS, but they do not look like the primary brand palette from the homepage’s overall visual tone; they are more likely tied to media/tool states, highlights, or embedded UI elements rather than the main marketing shell.

So for a design-system translation, I would treat these as secondary utility accents, not Eden’s core brand colors:
